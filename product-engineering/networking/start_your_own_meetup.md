# Start your own meetup
If you can't find any meetup that you like, start your own.
This in itself is product that is improving the market of networking events.

The meetup is how you envision, and you can invite people to it.

You will be the center of networking and can organically monetize it.
Monetize it off of the user drive to want to build a startup and network with like-minded people.
2-birds with 1-stone. Product, and networking.

# The Open Company
An open-source company that is open to the public.

Inherit a toastmasters-like model and structure.
- There are paid members, and free guests.
- An executive team that is elected by the paid members.
- Potentially partner with Toastmasters to offer public speaking training. Toastmasters beneifits with an influx of new members, and we benefit from their training programs.

# Session Structure
- Learning Product building
-- Paid speakers as well as the community members can present.
- Networking
-- open floor networking/hangout.
- Paid members can present problems they want to solve, and people who want to solve the same problem can form a founding group.
-- Being under the umbrella of the open company, they can work on their product and get help from the community, by getting the community to validate/test their product idea, give real user feedback, and help them build their product.
