# Landing page

This is the bridge between your marketing efforts and your product building efforts. 

Its the best first thing to work on since its something that readily can show the market your product concept and value prop. 
It is what they will be seeing or visiting besides ads before they decide they want your product.

Even for yourself, it captures the look and feel, the idea, the vision of your product as you build it.

The landing page is one of the easier things to work on and is significant in its role.
