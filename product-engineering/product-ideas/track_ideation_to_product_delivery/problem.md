# Problem Document - Ideation to Product Delivery

## Central problem
There is currently no visibility for me to take my idea documents and track the point where an idea is first noted down
to when I actually deliver that idea as a product in the market. There is no tracking of the stage of implementation 
an idea is currently at. 

Without this visibility I lost the idea of where I really am in my work and what i've actually been able to accomplish 
so far. This is especially true for soloprenuers who start out doing everything themselves. It is very easy
to get lost in the piles of work and feel burnt out or dissapointed by not seeing any reward from all the work and 
resources invested into a project.

Just like climbing a mountain, climbers need a way to track their distance to the summit. This keeps them motivated and
on task especially for long-lived endeavours.


## Current Best Solutions
Chatgpt describes this as a "personalized product lifecycle tracking system that helps solopreneurs move from ideation 
to market delivery while maintaining visibility, motivation, and organization."

It identifies existing products to be only partial to this vision.
1. Notion, Trello, ClickUp, Asana – Great for project management but lack event-based state tracking from ideation to launch.
2. Jira, Linear – Too heavyweight and enterprise-focused for solopreneurs.
3. Apple Notes, Markdown Files, Obsidian – Great for note-taking but lack structured progress tracking.
4. Kanban Boards (Trello, Github Projects) – Useful but don’t automatically map work across product lifecycle stages.

# Observations
People usually have a place where they write their ideas as it comes to mind. For me, it is my iPhone notes app and

My desktop /product-ideas folder as markdown files. When the idea becomes created for the first time. 
The lifecycle is at its first official stage of ideation. This is an event based trigger of one state of the product
delivery lifecycle. The other states may be as follows:
# Ideation (thinking, describing, and justifying the product)
- demand/problem-solution validation of value
- problem documents
- solution and proposal documents

# Implementation (building the product)
- technical feasibility
- proto-typing, POC, MVP
- hosting
- analytics
- release versioning

# Representation (wrapping the product in appeal)
- product demo videos
- product site and landing pages

# Distribution (marketing the product for attention)
- omni-channel social media postings
- sales

# Ideal solution
Courtesy of Chatgipitee:
A dedicated tool that automatically tracks your ideas through different lifecycle phases and provides visibility into progress.
Core Features
✅ Unified Idea Capture – Allow capturing ideas from Markdown, Notes apps, or voice recordings and structuring them automatically.
✅ Lifecycle Tracking – Assign ideas to predefined stages (Ideation → Implementation → Representation → Distribution).
✅ Automatic Stage Progression – Detect when an idea moves from a rough concept to a structured document to an MVP, etc.
✅ Visual Progress Map – Show how many ideas are in each stage and track completion rates.
✅ Motivational Tracking – Provide a “distance to summit” tracker like climbers use to track milestones.
✅ Time & Resource Investment Logging – Track effort spent on each phase and compare it to outcomes.
✅ Personal Dashboard & Reporting – Summarize which ideas have moved forward, how much time is spent per stage, and which areas need attention.
