# Problem Document - Omni-channel/social Media Content Creation

## Central problem
I don't want to spend the time creating my posts and making sure they are all formatted and grammatically correct, 
then also optimizing them per the platform algorithm. Then multiplying this activity by the frequency I want to 
be active on this platform. 

## Problem characteristics
Number of users this problem applies to (high | mid | low): mid
Frequency a user will experience this problem (high | mid | low): high

## Current Best Solutions
ChatGPT Product report:
Existing Solutions & Their Limitations
While there are some products that address parts of this problem, no single tool fully automates omni-channel content creation from daily activities. Below are the closest solutions:

Product	What It Does	Limitations
| **Product**               | **What It Does** | **Limitations** |
|--------------------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Jasper AI**           | AI-generated social media posts based on prompts and brand voice. | Does not automatically extract content from daily activities. |
| **Copy.ai**             | AI-powered content writing, including social media posts. | Requires manual input and lacks real-time automation. |
| **Hootsuite / Buffer**  | Schedules and posts content across platforms. | Does not create content; only handles publishing. |
| **Taplio (for LinkedIn)** | AI-suggested LinkedIn posts with scheduling. | Limited to LinkedIn; requires manual approvals. |
| **FeedHive**            | AI-powered social media management & analytics. | No automatic extraction from user activities. |
| **Zapier + ChatGPT**    | Can automate content creation and posting with workflows. | Requires manual setup and lacks a learning approval system. |



# Observations

# Ideal solution
I want to just have an AI agent or assistant know what my activities are in the day or what I am building, who I am 
reading about or learning from books, and what my notes are to know what my outlook on things are. Then using that
raw information tranform it into an optimal format for the target platform.

It will do this on a basis of specified active frequency, then it will create content for me to review per that 
frequency. e.g. per day, it will ask me to approve, reject, or modify the content it creates. Then it will post if approved
or rework if not.

ChatGPT suggestion
What Would the Ideal Solution Look Like?
A Personalized AI Content Assistant that:
✅ Monitors your activities (e.g., what you're building, reading, writing).
✅ Extracts key insights and turns them into engaging, platform-specific posts.
✅ Learns your style and writing tone over time.
✅ Provides drafts for approval (Approve, Modify, Reject, Regenerate).
✅ Automatically posts at optimal times based on engagement trends.
✅ Cross-posts and adapts content across multiple platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Medium, etc.).
