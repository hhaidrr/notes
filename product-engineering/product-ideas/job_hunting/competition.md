# Competition Analysis
| Tool           | Main Feature                        | Platforms Supported            | Unique Selling Point                    |
|----------------|------------------------------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| LazyApply      | Automated application filling       | LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter| Bulk apply, AI answers, tracking        |
| JobCopilot     | Autofill & submit applications      | Multiple                      | Customization, exclusion lists          |
| Loopcv         | AI-powered screening & automation   | Multiple                      | Selective, targeted applications        |
| Simplify       | Autofill, resume builder            | Multiple                      | 1-click apply, AI resume builder        |
| Huntr          | Application tracking                | Multiple                      | Comprehensive tracking                  |
| Teal           | AI autofill, personalized answers   | Multiple                      | Review/edit before submit               |
| Massive        | AI auto-apply                      | Multiple                      | Effortless, AI-driven submissions       |
| Bulk Apply     | Bulk automation, tracking           | Multiple                      | Custom profiles, resume feedback        |
| ApplyPass      | Tailored mass applications          | Multiple                      | 200-400 applications/month              |
| AI Blaze       | Resume/cover letter generation      | Any                           | GPT-4, free, multi-tasking              |
| Apply Hero AI       | Auto-apply      | Any                           
## Good
They have a good referral program. Each referral gives you 250 free applications.
Their Resume upload UX is good.

## Bad
- Minimal transparency on what's happening with my application.
If I am trusting some black box to do the work for me, I want to know what it's doing.
I have to wait a day for my manually picked job auto-apply to begin. Why is it taking that long? If there are other 
people in queue before me, I want to know how many.
- The auto-apply toggle is on and it keeps blinking "Applying" but nothing is happening. My pending tab is still 
empty. I want to know if it's actually applying or not.
