# Fixing bugs as fast and efficiently as possible
- The anatomy of a bug
-- The symptoms
-- The root cause

1. comprehension
- Read and develop an initial frame of understanding from the bug report. What is wrong, what is the symptom, what should happen.

2. reproduction
- Experience the bug for yourself
- This is your first frame of experience

3. Find the root
- When you reproduce the bug (step 2) you are at the outermost layer of the bug's scope of effect.
- We have the first frame of understanding and the first frame of experience
- To change our frames of experience in a guided and targeted way rather than making random changes, 
we need to develop our frame of understanding. Developing our understanding will be what develops our experience
- How do we develop understanding? targeted experiments.
