# Pre-Sales

What to do before the product actually exists. Or is currently being built.
Measure the market, validate the idea, and get early adopters.

1. Interviews (10-30 people)
    - Personal network
    - LinkedIn
    - Online communities (Reddit, Facebook, etc.)

    - Listen, understand the pain pints, don't pitch
    - Focus on why it is painful, where does the pain come from
    - Guage wilingness to pay: toward the end, gently probe, if a tool could genuinely save you (x pain), how valuable would that be to you? Would you consider paying a small monthly fee for it?

    Record & Analyze: Take detailed notes. Look for recurring themes, specific frustrations, and the exact language they use. This language will be priceless for your marketing copy.
