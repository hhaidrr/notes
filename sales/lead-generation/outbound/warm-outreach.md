# Warm Outreach
Instead of reaching out to people who don't know you, warm outreach is about reaching out to people who have some familiarity with you or your company. 
This can be through mutual connections, previous interactions, shared interests, friends and family.

# Call Structure
1. Intro, just a ask them about life, connect, rapport, whats new, etc.
2. Do you have time to chat?
3. Would you be interested in what I do?
4. Do you know anyone who might be interested in what I do?
5. Offer to do it for free in exchange for reviews and referrals.
