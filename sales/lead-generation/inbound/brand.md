# Creating a Brand

## Capturing the Trademark
1. Trademark Search: This is non-negotiable. If your name is legally problematic, nothing else matters.

2. Domain Name (.com): Get it immediately if available.

3. Key Social Media Handles: Secure them as soon as you choose a name.

Lean Brand Identity: While a full brand style guide is ideal, as a solopreneur, you might start with a "mini" style guide in your head or a simple document:
What are my core brand words?
What 2-3 colors will I use?
What 1-2 fonts will I use consistently?
What's my tone of voice?
